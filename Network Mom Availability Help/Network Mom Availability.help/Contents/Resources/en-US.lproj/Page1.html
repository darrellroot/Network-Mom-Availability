Audio Alerts

Audio alerts are disabled by default. To enable audio alerts, select  Network Mom Availability -> Preferences .

  • Set the audio alert frequency to “one minute” or “five minute” rather than the default network.

  • Choose your preferred audio alert. Hit “test” to sample the sound.

You may need to adjust your Macintosh volume. If you choose “system beep” you can control that in  System Preferences -> Sound -> Select an alert sound .
