Monitors

A monitor has several elements:

  • Address: (required) This could be an IPv4 or IPv6 address. This is the actual target of the monitoring.

  • Test Type: (required) This could be ICMPv4 or ICMPv6.

  • Hostname: (optional) This could be automatically detected by Network Mom Availability at the time of monitor creation, or manually entered by the user. While DNS->IP address mappings may change over time, Network Mom Availability does not automatically update the hostname or IP address. If the IP address of a monitored target changes, you must delete the monitor and create a new one with the updated address.   Map->Arrange Monitors  sorts monitors by hostname.

  • Comment: (optional) This descriptive text is included on the map and in alert messages. It can help support personnel understand the type of device or the impact of it being down.
