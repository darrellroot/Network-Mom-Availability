99.9% “three nines” availability is 86.4 seconds of downtime per device per day. This is easy with the discipline of measuring availability and fixing problems.

99.99% “four nines” availability is 8.64 seconds of downtime per device per day. This is hard but possible in a solid corporate network.

99.999% “five nines” availability is 25.92 seconds of downtime per device per  month . If you ping test every 30 seconds, missing one ping means the device has failed for the month. This is only achievable in hardened corporate datacenters.


The first step to achieving high availability is to measure it. That is why  Network Mom Availability  is here.
